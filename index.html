My name is Caleb Farrelly and I'm a Master's Student at Cornell University studying Aerospace Engineering.

I have extensive experience in rocket propulsion, CNC machining, and engineering leadership. Most of my time at Cornell was spent on the Cornell Rocketry Team, where I started the team's hybrid rocket propulsion program, and then lead the entire team, culminating in winning 3rd place at the 2025 IREC competition. Although my specialization is in Propulsion, I have a passion for all aspects of engineering, and I have been recently exploring electronics design and development. I have completed internships at Vast Space, SpaceX, Astranis, Duke Photonics Laboratory, Helios Engineering, and Bumblebee AI (now BloomX).

Programs: NX, ANSYS Mechanical & Fluent CFD, SolidWorks, Fusion 360 (CAD & CAM), MATLAB, Altium, LaTeX
Skills: Fluid system design & analysis, cryogenics, harnessing, CNC mill & lathe, welding, additive manufacturing, soldering
