Designed and 3D printed a mortar launched rocket in an attempt to break the sound barrier with a black powder rocket engine.

Project Details

Supersonic Rocket Project: This ambitious personal project involved designing a 3D printed rocket with the goal of achieving supersonic speeds using a black powder engine. The project required careful aerodynamic design, structural analysis, and innovative manufacturing techniques using 3D printing technology.

Educational Outreach: Recognizing the importance of inspiring the next generation of engineers, I established a comprehensive rocketry outreach program. This initiative engaged over 70 disadvantaged middle and elementary school students, providing hands-on experience with rocket fuel preparation, Estes rocket construction, and launch activities.
